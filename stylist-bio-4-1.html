Emma

Hi, I’m Emma, and I’m here to make your salon experience not just about great hair, but also about feeling at ease, relaxed, and, most importantly, heard. With me, it’s not just a haircut, it’s a conversation. I’m known for being approachable and relatable, always striving to make sure you feel comfortable in my chair, whether it’s your first visit or your fiftieth. What sets me apart is my ability to empathize and connect with all kinds of people. Growing up in a big family taught me the value of listening, understanding, and laughing together and I bring that same energy to every appointment. It’s all about creating an atmosphere where you can just be yourself, share your hair dreams (or struggles), and leave feeling like your true self, only better!

I’m passionate about working with all types of hair. Whether it’s curly, straight, thick, thin, or somewhere in between. There’s no “one-size-fits-all” approach when it comes to hair, and that’s why my goal is simple: I don’t have a target client. I love seeing and working with every hair type, so whether you’re looking for a dramatic transformation or a subtle refresh, I’m excited to get to know your hair and make it shine. Humor is a big part of who I am, so don’t be surprised if you catch me cracking a joke during your appointment. After all, why not have some fun while making your hair fabulous? I can’t wait to meet you, learn your story, and help you look and feel your absolute best.
